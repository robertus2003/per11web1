Project 1 :

Implement Waste Management,reduce,reuse,recycle.

Problems

The ocean is an essential component of the earth's ecosystem, playing a critical role in regulating global climate and supporting a wide range of marine life. However, with the increasing human activities and the consequent generation of waste, the ocean is facing a severe pollution problem

Solution

One of the most effective ways to manage waste in the ocean is to reduce, reuse, and recycle waste materials. This approach involves reducing the amount of waste generated by adopting sustainable practices such as using eco-friendly products, reducing packaging, and avoiding single-use plastics..

Problems

Therefore, managing waste in the ocean is a crucial step towards environmental conservation, and it requires a comprehensive approach involving all stakeholders, including governments, non-governmental organizations, communities, and individuals
